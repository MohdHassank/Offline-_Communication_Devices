# Research and References

This document lists the key research sources, technical foundations, and supporting tools used while designing and developing the Offline Communication Device.

---

## Research Sources

- **LoRa Technology for Disaster Communication**  
  Source: IEEE Xplore (research papers on LoRa-based emergency networks)

- **NDMA (National Disaster Management Authority) Communication Report – 2024**  
  Used to understand real-world communication challenges during disasters.

- **Low Power Wide Area Networks (LPWAN) in Emergency Scenarios**  
  Various technical articles studying LoRa and LPWAN use in disaster response.

- **Existing Emergency Communication Systems – Global Rescue**  
  Studied limitations of current systems like satellite phones and emergency radios.

---

## Tech Foundations

- **LoRa Module – Reyax RYLR998**  
  Reference: Official Datasheet and Documentation

- **ESP32 Microcontroller**  
  Reference: Espressif Official Documentation

- **Arduino Nano**  
  Reference: Arduino Official Documentation

These official resources were used to understand hardware behavior, pin configuration, power usage, and communication protocols.
